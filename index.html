最後の帰還

地球が変わってしまった。それでも、世界に灯りは残っていた。

A cinematic 3DCG film project. A lone figure stands under a single light in a world transformed — a still image that holds the weight of an entire film.

HOW IT'S MADE

The Process

VIEWPORT · WIREFRAME
FINAL RENDER

01

Concept

Every work begins with a feeling — a quality of light, a silence before something happens. Narrative forms around atmosphere, not plot.

02

Modeling

Built in Blender. Form is studied, then distorted just enough to suggest something more than familiar. Or less. The line is intentional.

03

Lighting

Lighting is the real subject. A face in near-darkness. A rim of cold blue. The amber of something burning offscreen. Shadow as character.

04

Texture

Materials layered until the boundary between CG and photograph becomes uncertain. Skin that has weight. Metal with memory.

05

Composition

Framed like film. Wide lenses, compression, shallow depth. The image should feel like a still from something you almost remember watching.

06

Post

DaVinci Resolve / After Effects でグレーディング、グレイン、レンズ収差を加える。引き算の美学。
